Welcome to the African American Society of Rockland County: Linking/Preserving the Cultures of the African Diaspora (AAHS) website.

Rockland County is indeed the smallest county in the state of New York, yet its size is magnified by the significant history of the people of African descent. The AAHS is the premier organization in the county uncovering this little known history and preserving this information for future generations
